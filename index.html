🍋 Meet Tanvir Hossain

I’m Tanvir Hossain, a CSE student at Daffodil International University. What started as looking after my family’s mango garden during summer has grown into Tanvir Worlds — a small-batch, direct-from-farm brand. Every fruit you order is grown, harvested, and packed by people I know personally. No middlemen, no carbide ripening — just the mangoes I grew up eating.
